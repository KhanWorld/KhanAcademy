   GMAT Preparation

Sal has done all 404 problem solving and data sufficiency problems in "The Official Guide for GMAT Review" by the Graduate Management Admission Council (which you should buy) in the 100+ videos below.

We recommend that you buy the book and take try to do as many problems as possible. Then you should watch the following videos to have them explained. We've gotten feedback that it is sometimes helpful to watch the videos for questions you understand just to see how Sal tackles them.

If you have the self-discipline to do the practice problems, review your work and watch the videos, we think you will be as prepared as anyone. You should view these videos as your on-demand personal tutor.

If you need to review (or learn) underlying mathematical concepts covered on the GMAT, we have over 700 videos on general math topics in our video library.
